About Nicky G. Computers

Your trusted technology partner in Wa, Ghana

Our Story

Located in the heart of Wa, Upper West Region, Nicky G. Computers has been serving the community with reliable technology solutions and services. We are your one-stop shop for computers, phones, repairs, and various IT services.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to bridge the digital divide in our community by providing quality technology products and services at affordable prices. Today, we continue to serve hundreds of satisfied customers across the Upper West Region.
